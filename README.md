# Avarice & Gluttony
You are an empty shell who can only be happy through consumption and possession of moolah.
Scavenge for resources, combine them to make new ones, consume them to satiate your gluttony, or sell them to satiate your avarice!
Or just keep your items and marvel at what you've obtained through less than moral means.
There MIGHT be a recipe book or compendium of items. (There won't be.)
(Also, I got too ambitious. The crafting system and secret objective aren't implemented yet. Feel free to look at the code and see what could've been.)

### AI Use
The main game loop, the part that comes after the opening monologue, but not the smaller methods. Any all design choices, narrative choices, and flavor text are my own.
