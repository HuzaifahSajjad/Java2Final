# Avarice & Gluttony
You are an empty shell who can only be happy through consumption and possession of moolah.
Scavenge for resources, combine them to make new ones, consume them to satiate your gluttony, or sell them to satiate your avarice!
Or just keep your items and marvel at what you've obtained through less than moral means.
There MIGHT be a recipe book or compendium of items.
